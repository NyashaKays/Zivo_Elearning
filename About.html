Why does Zivo E-Learning exist?


Allowing millions access to an abundance
of knowledge anytime anywhere !


After noticing the change in work life balance that was brought about by the advent of the covid-19 pandemic
our founder Mr. Nyashadzashe T Katsidzira realized the need to decouple education and physical infrastructure.
Being an aspiring Software Engineer he decided to put his newly acquired skills to the test by providing a
home for users of any age, background and ethnicity to be able to access knowledge from any field without
the restraints that come with physical interactions. Thus Zivo E-learning platform came to be.
